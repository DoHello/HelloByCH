Ted Zhang, CEO and co-founder of DerbySoft, invited to discuss at "The 3rd CEO Summit of Global Online Travel Agents—Smart Travel, Connect Future"Publishing Time:September 25, 2014

"The 3rd CEO Summit of Global Online Travel Agents—Smart Travel, Connect Future" was hosted by the Sichuan Tourism Administration and Mianyang People's Government on September 16. The conference attracted many professionals such as Google, Baidu, Facebook, Abacus, Ctrip, Qunar, Hana Tour, JTB, and DerbySoft. Any worldwide famous websites, OTAs, airlines, travel agencies, and many senior executives from relative industries also came to Mianyang Beichuan to attended conference, discussing new challenges and new opportunities from internet technologies for the global tourism industry.
DerbySoft's Co-founder & CEO, Mr. Ted Zhang was invited to join the Dialogue to discuss "Big Data Era".
Dialogue：
Ted Zhang, Co-founder & CEO, DerbySoft
Changren Wei, President & Lead analyst, CTCNN.com
Libin Hou, Executive Vice President of Marketing Research, Baidu
Fang Cai，VP, Greater China, ComScore
Moderated by：
Viktor Mayer, 《Big Data Era》authur, Professor at the university of Oxford

Ted Zhang has more than ten years' experience in the Internet and Travel Information Management space. He established DerbySoft with Feng Ou, the company's CTO, in December, 2002. He has been invited numerous times to be a guest speaker at the China Hotel Marketing Summit and China Travel Distribution Summit organized by TravelDaily. He has also been interviewed and quoted by CBNweekly, Nanfang Metropolis Daily, Oriental Morning Post, Business China, and TravelDaily.
